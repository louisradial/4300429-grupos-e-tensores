    Seja
    \begin{align*}
        [\noarg, \noarg] : \mathbb{R}^3 \times \mathbb{R}^3 &\to \mathbb{R}^3\\
                                      (\vetor{a},\vetor{b}) &\mapsto \vetor{a}\times \vetor{b},
    \end{align*}
    então \((\mathbb{R}^3, [\noarg, \noarg])\).
